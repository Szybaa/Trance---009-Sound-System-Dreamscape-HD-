Some Milky Way Facts:

  • The Milky Way is a barred spiral galaxy.
  • It contains over 100 billion stars.
  • The Milky Way is about 100,000 light-years in diameter.
  • The galactic center is about 26,000 light-years away from Earth.
  • The Milky Way is estimated to be about 13.2 billion years old.
